Diet and Habitats
The Ibis Birds have a diet consisting of mainly insects; However they also eat Crabs, Snails, Worms, Fish, Frogs, Lizards and small mammals. Ibis Birds are widespread in Sub-Saharan Africa but are also largely clustered in other ares of the world such as Australia.

The Australian Ibi bird has white feathers compared to other types of Ibi Birds.

African Ibi birds are flying in a pair.

African Ibi birds can be found in a multitude of terrains such as grasslands or wet ares.
